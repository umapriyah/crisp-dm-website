Problem Statement

The objective of this analysis is to explore customer purchasing behavior using the Online Retail dataset. The goal is to identify valuable customers, uncover sales patterns, and provide insights that can help improve business strategies.

1. Business Understanding

The business seeks to enhance marketing strategies by gaining a deeper understanding of customer spending habits and preferences. Identifying key customer segments allows for more targeted and effective campaigns.

2. Data Understanding

The dataset contains detailed transaction information, including invoice number, product description, quantity, price, customer ID, and country. Exploring and understanding these variables is essential for accurate analysis.

3. Data Preparation

Data preprocessing involved removing missing customer IDs, filtering out cancelled transactions, and creating a new column, TotalPrice, to facilitate spending analysis. This ensures clean and reliable data for modeling.

4. Modeling

Customers were grouped based on total spending to classify them into high-value, medium-value, and low-value segments. This segmentation helps focus marketing efforts on the most valuable customers.

5. Evaluation

The results were assessed to ensure the identified segments are meaningful and actionable. The analysis successfully highlights patterns that can guide targeted marketing strategies.

6. Deployment

The insights are presented through this website, providing a user-friendly interface for decision-makers to access and apply the results in business planning.
