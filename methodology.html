CRISP-DM Phases

1. Business Understanding

Define the project objectives, business goals, and success criteria to ensure the data analysis addresses real-world needs.

2. Data Understanding

Collect the necessary data, explore its content, and assess its quality to identify potential issues or opportunities.

3. Data Preparation

Clean, transform, and organize the data so it is ready for analysis or modeling.

4. Modeling

Apply analytical or data mining techniques to discover patterns, trends, and insights.

5. Evaluation

Assess the results to ensure they meet the original business objectives and are reliable for decision-making.

6. Deployment

Present the findings in a usable format, integrate them into business processes, or use them to guide actions.

CRISP-DM is widely adopted because of its flexibility, structured approach, and applicability across different industries, including retail, finance, healthcare, and marketing. Its iterative nature allows teams to revisit and refine each phase for continuous improvement.
